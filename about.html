All About Me!

👋 Hi there!

I am Buhle Damane

Front-end developer, based in South Africa

I was born and raised in South Africa, where I discovered my love for sports and books at a young age. Now, as I continue my academic journey, I'm proud to have earned my Bachelor of Laws degree from the University of Venda. Currently, I'm working towards a Master's degree in Laws at the University of South Africa. This transition signifies my ongoing commitment to justice and learning. Following my passion for justice and the intricacies of law, I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Law (LLB) at the esteemed University of Venda.

However, it was during my final year at university that I stumbled upon another calling: technology. Intrigued by its potential to transform industries and drive innovation, I decided to explore this newfound interest further. This decision led me to embark on a journey with SheCodes, where I immersed myself in the world of front-end development. Through dedication and hard work, I honed my skills and discovered a whole new realm of possibilities.

Today, armed with both legal expertise and technical know-how, I find myself at the exciting intersection of law and technology. My story is a testament to the power of following one's passions fearlessly and embracing the unexpected twists and turns that life presents. As I continue to navigate this ever-evolving landscape, I remain committed to making a meaningful impact, both in the legal realm and the world of technology. Join me on this journey as we strive to push boundaries and shape the future together.
